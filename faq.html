FAQ's

1. We already have a data base center. Can we have the data Locally?

Yes, it’s possible.
However, special technical arrangements need to be done.

2. What is your experience in Saudi Market?

We are entering the market now. However, we know to serve and satisfy big companies in Switzerland where the market structure and client needs have shown to be complex.

3. Where are your application development centers based?

In Switzerland.

4. Do you guarantee the security and confidentiality of our intellectual property?

Yes! NDA clause is covered in the IP contract, and NDA is also signed between jobdesk® and the customer specifying the same. The same clause is also covered in the contract signed between jobdesk® and its employees. In addition, we audit out processes and train employees on a regular basis. These efforts ensure that our customers’ intellectual property is always secure.

5. How do you handle change request from your customers?

Change requests are reviewed by a project manager together and you will be provided with a time and cost estimate. Because we have a very flexible software architecture and a simple organizational hierarchy, we manage to deliver in very short timespans.

6. Who owns the data of the jobdesk® software?

All data within the application is owned by the client. The client is also responsible for the published content and is required to be compliant with the regulatory framework he is operating.

7. We are a holding company managing few sister companies and expanding.
What is your solutions for such companies?
Will each company be in a separate database or how will you deal with it?

jobdesk® can handle multiple companies in one application. Employees are assigned to the respective company. Payroll can be done for each company separated, within the same application. jobdesk® is designed for enterprises. If you decide to see all employees in the same application, we’ll run it all on the same database -means less maintenance for your IT if you decide to host your data on your servers (locally).

8. Who has network access to my projects at jobdesk®?

Only those developers who are assigned to your project team have password-protected permission to access your projects on the jobdesk® network. Passwords are changed on a regular basis in accordance with our strict security policies.

9. How do you price your services?

Pricing is created on a project basis or a retainer schedule. Since the resource need of each company are different, pricing for each project is customized and presented in the MSA or SOW. In general, the cost for the expert resources are higher than hiring a traditional employee, however the benefits include a faster time to learning ramp-up and faster time to delivery of software, which easily offsets the costs. Our past and current clients see the value in the price they pay.

10. What training packages do you offer?

We tailor our training to meet your needs so we can offer training by the hour or by the day, online or at your offices, and on the topic of your choice. You just tell us what you want and we tailor a training package to suit you, without any charges.We also provide you with manuals and instructions via videos on how to use the software. Our customers are never alone.

11. Can jobdesk® recruiter integrate to our company site?

jobdesk® provides mutliple way to connect with other system, i.e. to connect with your website, time system, etc..

12. Can we connect jobdesk® recruiter with LinkedIn?

In the circumstances where you are using job slots on LinkedIn to publish job ads you could decide to re-direct the candidates to a registration form on the ‘work with us’ page of your website. This is a way of reinforcing your Employer Branding, of collecting information about your candidates in a more structured way, giving you the chance to perform more advanced searches of candidates, it is much more powerful than the functionality on LinkedIn (thus reducing your screening time).

13. Does jobdesk® work on mobile device? Is it available on-premise or cloud based?

jobdesk® is available cloud based or can be run within your internal network. jobdesk® is mobile friendly and can be used on any device, using any browser. Complex features like backoffice payroll processing are not suitable to be used on small mobile screens.

14. What happens to my data if I decide to stop using jobdesk® recruiter?

We can provide with the basic data as a zip-File for download. You are the owner of the data.

15. What are the hardware and software requirements to run jobdesk®?

A simple device (PC, Mac, Laptop, Tablet or Smartphone) with a browser.

16. What type of support do you provide? And how much does it cost?

Whichever subscription you choose to you will have access to our customer support via email included in the fee. If you prefer we can also enable a level of premium support by phone. We have also created an online guide available 24 hours a day with all the information that you need.

17. How does the In-recruiting free trial work?

The free 14-day trial provides access to all of the jobdesk® Recruitment functionality, without any obligation to buy. At the end of the trial period you can decide whether to purchase a plan that suits your needs, or whether to stop using the service (you can still download all of the information and CVs that you’ve collected).If this is of interest, we’d like to draw your attention to our free 14-day trial.

18. Role Based Access Control

jobdesk® supports role-based authorization, which means users have limited access to data and functionality. Role Based Access Control (RBAC) protects the front-end and back-end data and system resources by implementing access control restrictions. Users can only view and access information that they are supposed to see. For example, a recruiter might not have access to profiles of candidates for executive level positions or a junior interviewer might give a right to enter his inputs about a candidate, but not allowed to recommend hiring him. The administrator can define unlimited number of roles and can control access at a candidate, screen, action or data level.

19. Are there any data capacity limits?

No, there are no limits. However, data above 10GB will be charged additionally at a very low cost.

20. It is possible to send and receive emails in jobdesk® Recruiter?

Email can be sent and incoming email tracked through jobdesk® Recruiter, with all emails being tracked against the relevant contact, company or vacancy.

21. What management reporting tools are available in the system?

A standard set of management reports, charts and dashboards are pre-loaded with jobdesk® Recruitment. Users can also easily create their own using the wizards in the system

22. Does jobdesk® Recruitment have an applicant tracking system (ATS)?

Yes, jobdesk® has a very powerfull ATS, process based and process free, depends on the way your work!
